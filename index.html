Welcome to My HBO-ICT Journey

Since I was young, I’ve always been fascinated by technology—how things work, how they are built, and how they can change our lives. This natural curiosity led me to pursue the HBO-ICT program. I believe the dynamic and ever-evolving field of ICT is a perfect match for my interests and skills. From developing innovative software solutions to understanding the complexities of cybersecurity, ICT offers limitless opportunities to explore and grow.

As a first-year student in the HBO-ICT program, I have begun my journey into the world of technology. So far, I have learned the basics of HTML and CSS, which have helped me understand how websites are structured and styled. This foundational knowledge has sparked my interest in web development and how design can enhance user experience. Each new concept I encounter, from creating layouts to styling elements, encourages me to explore further and fuels my passion for ICT. I am excited to continue learning and developing my skills in this dynamic field.

Key Areas of Interest in ICT

  • Software Development: Building efficient, user-friendly applications.
  • Cybersecurity: Ensuring data privacy and protection.
  • Artificial Intelligence: Harnessing machine learning to solve complex problems.
