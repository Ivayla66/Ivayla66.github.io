Why I Choose ICT and Why It Suits Me

Since I was young, I’ve always been fascinated by technology—how things work, how they are built, and how they can change our lives. This natural curiosity led me to pursue the HBO-ICT program, a decision that aligns perfectly with my analytical skills, attention to detail, and ability to focus deeply on tasks. I believe that the dynamic and ever-evolving field of ICT offers limitless opportunities to explore and grow.

As a first-year student in the HBO-ICT program, I have begun my journey into the world of technology. So far, I have learned the basics of HTML and CSS, which have helped me understand how websites are structured and styled. This foundational knowledge has sparked my interest in web development and how design can enhance user experience. I thrive in environments where I can dive into complex problems and solve them meticulously, and each new concept I encounter only fuels my passion for ICT.

Key Areas of Interest in ICT

  • Software Development: Building efficient, user-friendly applications.
  • Cybersecurity: Ensuring data privacy and protection.
  • Artificial Intelligence: Harnessing machine learning to solve complex problems.
