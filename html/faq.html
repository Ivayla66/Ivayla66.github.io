Frequently Asked Questions

Here you can find answers to some common questions about my work and projects.

1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

To print a document, connect to the university's Wi-Fi network, find a printer through the HZ portal, and follow the instructions for sending your document to the selected printer.

2. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

You can scan documents using the multi-function printers located in the study areas. After scanning, send the document to your email or save it to a USB drive.

3. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

Visit the HZ webshop, create an account or log in, select the items or events you wish to purchase, and follow the payment instructions to complete your order.

4. How can you book a project space?

To book a project space, use the online reservation system available on the HZ website. Select the date and time you need, and confirm your booking.

5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Parking is available at HZ. You need to register your vehicle in the parking system and follow the parking regulations indicated on-site.
