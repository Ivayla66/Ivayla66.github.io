First Feedback

My first major feedback came from my SKC assignment, and it has had a big impact on how I approach my studies. The feedback was constructive, pointing out areas where I could improve, particularly in terms of clarity and organization of my work. I realized that I need to spend more time planning and structuring my assignments before diving into them.

It was encouraging to receive positive feedback as well, particularly about my research and analysis. This motivated me to continue working hard, but also to improve on my weaknesses. Since receiving this feedback, I have been more intentional about reviewing my work and seeking additional input from peers before submitting assignments.
