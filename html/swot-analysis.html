Personal SWOT Analysis

As I reflect on my journey, it's essential to assess my strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats (SWOT) to understand where I stand.

Strengths

One of my key strengths is my willingness to learn. Although I don't have a lot of experience in coding, I am always eager to tackle new challenges. I am also patient and persistent, which helps me when working through complex problems. Additionally, I am highly ambitious and set high standards for myself, which motivates me to continuously improve and achieve my goals.

Weaknesses

A key weakness is my limited experience in programming. Although I have completed an online Python basics course and have some exposure to HTML and CSS during my last two school years in Bulgaria, I still have a lot to learn. At times, I can also be overly ambitious, which makes it difficult to maintain a balance between perfectionism and progress.

Opportunities

The opportunities ahead of me are vast. Studying IT provides me with a platform to develop critical skills that are in high demand. As I continue my studies, I have access to resources, internships, and projects that can help me gain practical experience and build a strong portfolio. The growing tech industry offers numerous possibilities, and I plan to explore fields like software development, cybersecurity, or data science.
