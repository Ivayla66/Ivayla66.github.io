Study Choice

Choosing the right study path was a critical decision for me. After reflecting on my personal interests and goals, I decided to pursue a career in IT. Technology has always fascinated me, and I enjoy problem-solving and exploring new solutions. My aim is to gain the knowledge and skills necessary to contribute to the ever-growing tech industry and eventually work in fields like software development or cybersecurity.

After completing my studies, I hope to have a solid foundation in programming, systems analysis, and IT infrastructure. My long-term goal is to work on impactful projects that make a difference in people's lives by leveraging technology.
